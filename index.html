Our Story

Born from passion, each candle is made to spark joy and relaxation. Chivies Bellas began with a simple idea — that everyday moments deserve a little ritual. What started as a kitchen experiment on a rainy weekend became a small-batch candle brand rooted in calm, craft, and clean ingredients. Each candle is hand-poured with natural soy wax, lead-free wicks, and thoughtfully blended scents — designed to help you slow down, breathe deeply, and find your glow.
