About Chivis Bellas

Chivis Bellas was born from a love for creativity, fragrance, and elegant design. Each candle we create is hand-poured with care, inspired by nature’s beauty and crafted to bring warmth and personality into every space. At Chivis Bellas, we believe that beauty lives in the details. What started as a small passion project has grown into a heartfelt candle studio, where every piece is lovingly crafted by hand. Each candle carries its own story—infused with delicate fragrances, shaped by creativity, and designed to bring a sense of calm and charm into your home.

Our Mission

We aim to create beautifully scented candles that delight the senses, uplift the spirit, and transform everyday moments into something special—all while staying eco-conscious and handmade. Our mission at Chivis Bellas is to craft candles that do more than light a room—they create moments. We blend artful design with inviting scents to turn everyday spaces into calming sanctuaries. Each product is handmade with love, using eco-conscious materials, because beauty should never come at the expense of the earth.
